## What breaks the $50 feeling



### 1. Soft personalization / template smell

Named user, but large blocks could apply to anyone in a Wellbeing + Harvest month:

- Protect rest
- Say yes to the right opportunities
- Outer wealth reflects inner state

**Buyer need:** Why *this* palace, *these* stars, *this* decade year, *this* monthly stack — and how that shows up in real life categories.

### 2. Empty chart page hurts trust

“Focus palace is light on main stars; follow month activation moves” reads as a blank section after payment.

**Buyer need:** Reframe emptiness as meaning (method, reliance on flying stars, clarity theme), not an empty box.

### 3. Scorecard credibility problem

Career / Wealth / Relationships / Health all “rising” and all citing the **same driver** (Tan Lang Prosperity in Friends).

**Buyer need:** Distinct mechanisms per area, or drop the four-bar scorecard.

### 4. Calendar too coarse for premium

Week buckets (Strong / Strong / Caution / Neutral) fit free or low tier.

**Buyer need:** Dated windows (even 2–3 day bands) for talks, money, visibility, rest.

### 5. “Should I…?” feels soft / contradictory

Multiple items land on GOOD TIMING with caveats that could justify anything. Prosperity vs “new launches wait” is not hierarchy-resolved.

**Buyer need:** Hard ranking vs month priority — including clean **NO / WAIT**.

### 6. Closing is poetic, not operational

“Decide cleanly. Then rest into it” is beautiful but incomplete for $50.

**Buyer need:** The one decision, by when, success measure, and what next month inherits.

### 7. Year view is a map, not a story

Palace itinerary M1–M12 is nice but thin.

**Buyer need:** How Month 7 changes what Month 6 asked, and what to prepare for Month 8.

## Content wishlist (ideal $50 / year package)

What a believer expects when paying consultation money:

1. **Your month in one page** — palace, season, critical Si Hua, convergence, one move, one trap
2. **Why this is yours** — natal stars in focus + month overlays in plain English (explain sparseness if sparse)
3. **Lived scenes** — 3–4 short scenarios (work meeting, money decision, family text, sleep spiral) matching *this* activation
4. **Decision board** — 5 common choices with Hard Yes / Soft Yes / Wait / No + one sentence of chart reason each
5. **Allies & friction types** — Nobleman animal is good; add *one concrete ask* (coffee, intro, accountant, boundary)
6. **Body as dashboard** — weekly somatic map tied to chart pressure (e.g. Tian Ji loop → sleep)
7. **Bridge to next month** — what to close so next palace does not inherit unfinished debt
8. **For annual subscribers** — thin quarterly narrative synthesizing the last 3 months

## Suggested acceptance criteria (for improvement work)

A premium month is “done” when:

- [ ] Page 1 states **one move, one deadline, one success measure, one anti-pattern**
- [ ] Rarity / prior-match page explains **why this month is not generic**
- [ ] Empty-palace case has a **full meaning rewrite** (never a blank “no stars” page)
- [ ] Scorecard has **four distinct drivers** + vs-last-month trend
- [ ] Signal hierarchy resolves Prosperity vs caution without contradiction
- [ ] Work / Money / People each have **≤3 ranked chart-tied actions**
- [ ] Calendar has **≥3 dated windows** (not only week labels)
- [ ] Decision board includes at least one clear **WAIT** or **NO**
- [ ] Close includes **bridge to next month**
- [ ] Last month → this month memory is present for returning subscribers
